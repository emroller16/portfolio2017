Web Design

Cru at UConn
A Squarespace Site

Cru at UConn was looking to overhaul an existing blog to create a cleaner, more modern web presence. The site's main goals were to introduce prospective students to Cru at UConn, maintain an active blog, and provide updated information on the organizations events and programs. Further, the site had to be easy to maintain and update in the future.

A New Vision

  • With Cru, I helped create a vision for an entirely new site, with a new look & feel, new content, and a new site organization.
  • Together we planned planned out content for the site and organized into a simple architecture and navigation that would make information easy to find for a variety of audiences: prospective students, Cru members, and alumni.
  • I recommended Squarespace as a CMS because it would allow for easy updates and maintenance in the future and has both robust webpage and blog offerings.
  • Using Squarespace, I designed the site from start to finish with feedback from Cru at UConn.

Outcomes

  • A new clean, modern look.
  • Integration with Cru's social media presence.
  • An active blog with content from current students at Cru.
  • Easy client maintenance.
  • Simple, straightforward navigation.
