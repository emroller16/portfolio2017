Web Design

Core Lab
A UConn Wordpress Site

UConn Professor Leighton Core needed custom site for his Molecular Cell Biology lab that he could still maintain himself. He was looking for a site to showcase the lab's cutting edge research as well as attract prospective student researchers. Using UConn's instance of Wordpress, I created a custom site to achieve the lab's goals.

A Collaborative Process

  • Together with Dr. Core, we established needs and goals for the site.
  • I created wireframe concepts to flesh out a few ideas and get some feedback from Dr. Core.
  • Using Dr. Core's feedback, I created a test site in Wordpress.
  • After revisions with Dr. Core, we were ready for final publishing.
  • I provided a training guide as well as walked through admin functionality so that Dr. Core could continue to update his custom site.

Outcomes

  • Distinguished look and feel for information about the lab.
  • News blog with relevant updates.
  • Contact page for prospective student researchers to apply.
  • Prominent publications section.
  • Easy client updates.
