Digital Strategy

Social Media
UConn Community Outreach

UConn's Community Outreach program was looking to revamp their social media presence to create engaging and informative posts as well as share partner events and information with the community. Additionally, they sought to increase their followers on both Facebook and Instagram.

Engaging Posts

  • I created engaging posts to highlight and recognize specific community outreach programs.
  • Worked with several hashtag campaigns including #motivationmonday highlighting specific programs, #takeovertuesday promoting one program per week and #facesofCO which introduced a new student leader every week.
  • Created print and digital flyers for events within the community.
  • Made updates to the Community Outreach site as needed.
