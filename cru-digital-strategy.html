Digital Strategy

Social Media
Cru at UConn

Cru at UConn needed a new digital media presence. They were looking for a unified branding across multiple platforms including Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter that would feature engaging content and attract new followers.

A New Media Presence

  • I created a posting schedule to post consistently at least twice a week.
  • Focused the Facebook account to post information about events as well as sharing thought provoking articles as well as student blog posts.
  • Focused the Instagram account to post reminders about weekly meetings, recaps from weekly talks, and several hashtag campaigns.
  • Integrated Cru's social media presence with the Cru website to cross post across platforms.
